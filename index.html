This calculation does not include many other aspects of switching from a commercial software solution to a FOSS solution. In the following-section, you find a few common advantages and disadvantages of both, commercial and open-source software.

Commercial Software

Common Advantages

  • A lot of work is done for you
  • Feature-complete
  • May have better synergy with other commercial software

Common Disadvantages

  • High cost
  • Dependance on external factors
  • May or may not include backdoors and telemetry
  • May not synergize well with software outside the proprietary ecosystem

Open Source Software

Common Advantages

  • Free software
  • Freedom from external factors
  • Data security
  • May have better synergy with other open-source software

Common Disadvantages

  • Extra staff may be required to implement or organize features
  • Less functionality may be included in one software. Thus, two free solutions may be required to substitute for one commercial one.
  • Proprietary solutions may refuse to work and synergize with open standards
