Is Switching to Open Source Optimal in Your Situation?

The following criteria will be used to caculate the costs and savings of switching to an open-source solution:

Commercial Software

Common Advantages

  • A lot of work is done for you.
  • Feature-complete.

Common Disadvantages

  • High cost
  • Dependance on external factors
  • May or may not include backdoors and telemetry

Open Source Software

Common Advantages

  • Free software
  • Freedom from external factors
  • Data security

Common Disadvantages

  • Extra staff may be required to implement or organize features
  • Less functionality may be included in one software. Thus, two free solutions may be required to substitute for one commercial one.
